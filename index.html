About The Event

The annual technical event of AEC Coding Club - CODEWAR 4.0, organized under the banner of Udbhavanam - the annual technical fest of Assam Engineering College.

Codestellation: A 24 hours software based hackathon shall be conducted starting from introduction of problem statement to submission of the final software

Decode_Stack: Competitive Programming competition that will be conducted in two stages: the Debugging round, and the Final Code War

F.A.Q

  • What will the the time duration for the hackathon ?

    This will be a 24 hours software based hackathon.It will be started from introduction of problem statement to submission of the final software.

  • What will be the number of team members for the hackathon ?

    For the hackathon,each team can have 2-4 members.

  • When will the problem statement for the hackathon be released ?

    Problem statement will be given on the day of the hackathon.

  • How many rounds will be there in Decode_Stack ?

    The event Decode_Stack will be conducted in two stages: the Debugging round, and the Final Code War.

  • Is there any limitations for number of teams taking part from a particular institution?

    No,there is no such limit.

  • Can one participant take part in both the competetions ?

    Yes, one can attend both events.
