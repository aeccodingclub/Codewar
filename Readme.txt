CodeWar 3.0


Decode_Stack :- This Competitive Programming contest will be conducted in two stages: the Debugging Round, and the Final Code War. We shall provide participation certificates to all the participating students, goodies to the winner of the debugging round and finally, the three winners of the competition shall get prizes and certificates.


Codestallation :- A 24 hours Software based Hackathon shall be conducted starting from introduction of problem statement to submission of the final software. Refreshment shall be provided to the contestants. Judges from different industries and different academic backgrounds will decide the winner and thereafter prizes and certificates will be provided.


Date: 30th, 31st March and 1st, 2nd April.

Venue: Assam Engineering College, Guwahati, Assam, 781013
